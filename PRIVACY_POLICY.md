# Privacy Policy

TackleBox Manager does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All data entered in the app (fishing gear information, categories) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to external servers.

## Camera and Photo Library

The app uses the camera and photo library solely for AI-powered item scanning. Photos are sent to Google Gemini API for analysis and are not stored by the app.
